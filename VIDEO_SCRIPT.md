## TIPS FOR RECORDING:

1. **Pace**: Speak clearly and at a moderate pace - not too fast, not too slow
2. **Energy**: Maintain enthusiasm throughout - your excitement is contagious
3. **Screen Recording**: Use high-quality screen recording (1080p minimum)
4. **Cursor**: Use a cursor highlighter tool to make clicks visible
5. **Audio**: Use a good microphone - clear audio is crucial
6. **Transitions**: Use smooth transitions between sections
7. **Background Music**: Add subtle background music (low volume)
8. **Captions**: Consider adding captions for accessibility
9. **B-Roll**: Include some code snippets or architecture diagrams
10. **Call-to-Action**: End with clear next steps (visit site, check GitHub, etc.)
